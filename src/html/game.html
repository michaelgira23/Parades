What is Parades?

Pictionary + Charades = Parades
Object

Parades (or Charictionary) is a combination of Pictionary and Charades. The object of the game is for each team to score the most points by correctly guessing what their teammate is trying to draw and/or act out.

Game

Each round, the two teams will alternate and select a representative. The host of the game will secretly draw a random category and value for said representative to draw/act. The round ends after a certain time limit or if the team guesses correctly. If a team guesses correctly, then they earn a point. A team may only guess what the value is if the person drawing/acting is from their team.

If a team fails to guess what their teammate was trying to draw/act after the round, the other team has an opportunity to "steal" the round. They will be allowed one (1) guess what the other team was draw/acting out. If both teams fail, there will be no points given that round.

End

The game keeps on going until the game leader quits the game by pressing the "Leave Game" button. At that time, the team with the most points wins!
